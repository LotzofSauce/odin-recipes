Mississippi Roast

Description

The Mississippi Roast, a beloved Southern classic, is a dish steeped in tradition and flavor. This culinary gem boasts tender, succulent beef, slow-cooked to perfection with a medley of mouthwatering ingredients. The dish typically features a chuck roast, seasoned generously with ranch dressing mix, au jus gravy mix, butter, and tangy pepperoncini peppers, which impart a delightful kick and depth of flavor. As it simmers low and slow in the crockpot, the meat becomes melt-in-your-mouth tender, soaking up the rich, savory broth. The result is a dish that marries simplicity with sensational taste, making it a favorite for cozy family dinners or festive gatherings. Served alongside creamy mashed potatoes or buttery egg noodles, Mississippi Roast is sure to leave diners swooning and coming back for seconds. Its humble origins belie its incredible taste, proving that sometimes the most unassuming dishes are the most unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Ranch dressing mix
  • Au jus gravy mix
  • Butter
  • Jarred pepperoncini peppers

Steps

  1. Place the chuck roast in the bottom of your crock pot
  2. Sprinkle the ranch and au jus mixes over the top of the roast
  3. Add the butter and pepperoncini peppers
  4. Place the lid on top and cook over low heat for 8 hours or more
